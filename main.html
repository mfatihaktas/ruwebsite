My C.V. 


I am a Ph.D. student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Rutgers. I worked on both theoretical analysis and development of distributed systems.
I try to find practical solutions that show provably good performance for problems in request and resource scheduling in distributed storage/computing, anonymity in communication networks. 

For analysis, I mainly use tools from probabilistic modeling, queueing theory, order statistics, reinforcement learning.
For simulation and development, I use Python (Numpy, Scipy, Simpy, Mpmath, Matplotlib, TensorFlow), Linux clusters (MPI, OpenMP) and Cloud environment (EC2 mainly, MapReduce).
I keep the code for my work on github/mfatihaktas.
